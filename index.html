About Me

Sarah Mitchell is a classically trained vocalist with over 10 years of experience performing at venues across the country. Her rich, emotive voice has captivated audiences from intimate jazz clubs to grand concert halls.

Growing up surrounded by music, Sarah began singing at age 7 and went on to study vocal performance at the Royal Academy of Music. Her unique style blends traditional techniques with contemporary influences, creating a sound that is both timeless and fresh.

When not on stage, Sarah teaches private vocal lessons and works as a session vocalist for various recording projects.
