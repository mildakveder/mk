About Me

With a background in dance, theatre, and photo-modelling, I bring more than just vocals to the stage. My performances combine musical talent with expressive movement and genuine audience connection.

After years of balancing music with my career as an Executive Assistant, I made the leap to pursue music full-time in September 2025. Now I dedicate myself entirely to creating memorable musical experiences.

I speak five languages and sing in three, allowing me to connect with diverse audiences and bring an international flair to my repertoire.
