Background

With a background in dance and theatre, my performances combine musicality with expressive movement and genuine audience connection.

After years of balancing music with my career as an Executive Assistant, I made the leap to pursue music full-time in September 2025. Now I dedicate myself to creating memorable musical experiences.

Cosmopolitan flair allows me to connect with diverse audiences and bring international repertoire.
